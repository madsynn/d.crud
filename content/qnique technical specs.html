The Grace Qnique features a top-of-the-line bobbin system, including an easy-access bobbin case that makes things easy when you need to change or adjust your bobbin.
A high-speed automatic bobbin winder is built right into the machine. This makes refilling the bobbin a breeze! Just use the thread from one of the thread towers. The bobbin winder even shuts off automatically when the the bobbin is full!
The M-class bobbin is larger than standard sewing machine bobbins, allowing you to quilt longer before having to refill it.
